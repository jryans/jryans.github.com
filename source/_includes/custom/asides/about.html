About Me

I believe security is both a fundamental right and also our biggest challenge in today’s computing environment. I want to push the boundary of what’s possible through new research and open source tools that build upon techniques like symbolic execution, fuzzing, and formal verification.

I am currently a Master's student in Computer Science at EPFL in Lausanne, Switzerland.

I previously worked at Mozilla on developer tools for Firefox and Firefox OS.

I also worked on overhauling the Firefox CSS system using Rust and tech from Servo.
